\section{Yêu Xa Chỉ Bằng Tin Nhắn}
\begin{truyen}{Yêu Xa Chỉ Bằng Tin Nhắn}{Trying to Hold Long Distance Love with Messages Alone}
\chuhoa{C}{o cap doi nghi rang chi can nhan tin thuong xuyen la du giu mot moi tinh xa.}
\textit{(A couple thinks frequent texting alone can hold a long-distance relationship together.)}

Ho tuong cam xuc manh se duoc thay cho no luc song that.
\textit{(They think strong feelings can replace real effort.)}

Nhung tinh yeu xa can them su that, ke hoach, lich gap, va su on dinh trong cach song cua ca hai.
\textit{(But long-distance love also needs honesty, plans, meeting dates, and stability in both lives.)}

Khong co cau truc, nhung tin nhan ngot dan cung met.
\textit{(Without structure, sweet messages slowly become tiring.)}

\end{truyen}

\begin{baihoc}
Tinh yeu can cam xuc, nhung de giu duoc no can nhieu hon cam xuc.
\textit{(Love needs feeling, but keeping it needs more than feeling.)}
\end{baihoc}

\begin{ghinhoanh}
\textbf{Short English to remember:}

Love needs feeling, but keeping it needs more than feeling.

\end{ghinhoanh}

\section{Ghen Là Yêu Sâu}

\section{Quay Lại Quá Nhiều Lần}
\begin{truyen}{Quay Lại Quá Nhiều Lần}{Going Back Too Many Times}
\chuhoa{C}{o nhung cap doi chia tay roi quay lai lien tuc vi khong chiu noi don va co don.}
\textit{(Some couples break up and return again and again because they cannot bear pain or loneliness.)}

\textit{(They think repeated returns prove a special love.)}

\textit{(But repetition may not be great love. It may be a loop where no one learns the needed lesson.)}

Quay lai khong sai, nhung quay lai ma van song nhu cu thi chi keo dai vet thuong.
\textit{(Returning is not always wrong, but returning without change only stretches the wound.)}

\begin{baihoc}
Dung do tinh sau bang so lan quay lai. Hay do bang muc do ca hai da truong thanh hon chua.
\textit{(Do not measure depth by the number of returns. Measure it by whether both people have grown.)}
\end{baihoc}

\textit{(At some point, someone must face the truth that the other person wants to leave even though love remains.)}

Nguoi o lai tuong co the co gang them mot chut nua de doi y nguoi kia.
\textit{(The one left behind thinks a little more effort may change the other heart.)}

\textit{(But some relationships do not end because your effort was too small.)}

No mat vi mot ben khong con muon di tiep.
\textit{(They end because one side no longer wants to continue.)}

Buong tay luc do dau, nhung no giu em khoi viec phai van xin mot dieu khong con tu nguyen.
\textit{(Letting go hurts then, but it saves you from begging for something no longer freely given.)}

\end{truyen}

\begin{baihoc}
Yeu sau khong co nghia la giu duoc moi nguoi. Co khi no chi giup em buon ma van giu duoc pham gia.
\textit{(Loving deeply does not mean keeping everyone. Sometimes it only means grieving while keeping your dignity.)}
