\chapter*{Huong dan phat trien ban thao}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Huong dan phat trien ban thao}

\begin{leadbox}
Moi tinh huong duoi day dang o muc \textbf{de cuong mo rong}. Khi bien tap thanh sach hoan chinh, co the giu lai bo cuc 3 lop: tinh huong, doi thoai, bai hoc rut ra. Neu muon lam sach song ngu, moi truyen ngan co the them phien ban tieng Anh ngan o cuoi hoac chen song song tung doan ngan.
\end{leadbox}

\begin{leadbox}
De cuon sach co suc nang, moi chuong nen co it nhat 2 tinh huong theo goc nhin giao vien, 2 tinh huong theo goc nhin hoc sinh, 1 tinh huong theo goc nhin phu huynh, va 1 tinh huong co ket mo de nguoi doc thao luan. Co the chen them phan \textit{Hoi va nghi} o cuoi moi chuong cho giao duc tren lop.
\end{leadbox}

\begin{leadbox}
Ban thao hien tai da chia du 12 chuong va 120 tinh huong. Day la khung co the giao cho nguoi viet noi dung, nguoi dich song ngu, va nguoi thiet ke minh hoa lam song song ma khong can cho doi nhau.
\end{leadbox}
