\chapter*{Loi mo dau}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Loi mo dau}

\begin{leadbox}
Du thao \textit{Thay Xau - Thay Tot, Tro Xau - Tro Ngoan} duoc xay dung nhu mot khung noi dung co the mo rong thanh sach hoan chinh. Thay vi viet mot chieu theo kieu len lop, ban thao nay chon cach di tu tinh huong: mot loi noi, mot anh mat, mot cach xu ly trong lop co the de lai vet xuoc, nhung cung co the mo ra mot buoc ngoat cho ca thay va tro.
\end{leadbox}

\begin{leadbox}
12 chuong duoi day chia de tai theo nhung vung xung dot quen thuoc: ky luat, cong bang, quay cop, ap luc ban be, tieng noi cua phu huynh, su xau ho, that bai, va qua trinh truong thanh. Moi chuong co 10 tinh huong mau, du suc de bien tap tiep thanh truyen ngan, mau doi thoai song ngu, hoac bai doc thao luan trong lop hoc.
\end{leadbox}

\begin{leadbox}
Huong phat trien tiep theo la bo sung nhan vat xuyen suot, tang do day tam ly, va moi tinh huong co the di kem phan bai hoc, cau hoi thao luan, tu vung song ngu, hoac goi y hoat dong giao duc. O trang thai hien tai, day la mot khung du thao co the dung ngay de lap ke hoach viet sach.
\end{leadbox}
