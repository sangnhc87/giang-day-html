\section{Chi Dam Di Khi Biet Chac Ket Qua}
\begin{truyen}{Chi Dam Di Khi Biet Chac Ket Qua}{Moving Only When the Result Seems Certain}
\chuhoa{C}{o nguoi chi dam nop don, bat dau du an, hay thu mot huong moi khi thay kha nang thanh cong da ro rang.}
\textit{(Some people only dare apply, start a project, or try a new direction when success already looks clear.)}

Ho muon tranh cam giac that bai va xau ho.
\textit{(They want to avoid failure and embarrassment.)}

Nhung doi song hiem khi dua ra su chac chan truoc cho nguoi dang hoc di.
\textit{(But life rarely offers certainty in advance to someone who is still learning to move.)}

Neu chi dam di khi da chac, co the em se bo lo rat nhieu con duong chi mo ra trong luc dang di.
\textit{(If you only move when things are certain, you may miss roads that only appear while walking.)}

\end{truyen}

\begin{baihoc}
Rat nhieu co hoi khong thuong nguoi doi du chac. Chung thuong nguoi dam bat dau khi chua ro het.
\textit{(Many opportunities do not reward those who wait for certainty. They reward those who begin before seeing everything clearly.)}
\end{baihoc}

\begin{ghinhoanh}
\textbf{Short English to remember:}

Many opportunities reward those who begin

before everything is clear.

\end{ghinhoanh}

\section{Binh Yen Khong Den Tu Moi Thu Chac Chan}
\begin{truyen}{Binh Yen Khong Den Tu Moi Thu Chac Chan}{Peace Does Not Come from Total Certainty}
\chuhoa{S}{au nhieu lan meo met vi co gang giu moi thu on dinh, co nguoi bat dau hieu binh yen that khong nam o viec khoa chat tat ca.}
\textit{(After exhausting themselves trying to lock everything down, some people begin to understand that real peace does not come from tightening control over everything.)}

Binh yen that den khi minh biet thu gi co the rung ma van du suc dung cung no.
\textit{(Real peace comes when you know what may shake and still trust your ability to stand with it.)}

Tu cho do, su chac chan khong con la than duoc phai tim bang moi gia.
\textit{(From there, certainty is no longer an idol that must be worshiped at all costs.)}

No chi con la mot phan nho trong mot doi song du linh hoat, du thanh that, va du can dam de song tiep.
\textit{(It becomes only a small part of a life that is flexible, honest, and brave enough to continue.)}

\end{truyen}

\begin{baihoc}
Binh yen ben khong den tu viec giam het rung lac. No den tu viec lon du de dung cung rung lac.
\textit{(Inner peace does not come from removing every uncertainty. It comes from growing strong enough to stand with uncertainty.)}
\end{baihoc}

\begin{ghinhoanh}
\textbf{Short English to remember:}

Inner peace does not come from removing every uncertainty.

It comes from growing strong enough to stand with it.

\section{Di Duong Chua Chac Nhung Dung Hon}
\begin{truyen}{Di Duong Chua Chac Nhung Dung Hon}{Walking a Less Certain but More Honest Path}
\chuhoa{S}{au nhieu nam thich su chac chan, co nguoi bat dau chap nhan mot kieu song khac: khong ro het, nhung that hon voi minh.}
\textit{(After years of craving certainty, some people begin accepting a different way of living: less clear, but more honest.)}

Ho khong con doi tat ca den vi tri an toan moi buoc.
\textit{(They no longer wait for everything to stand in a safe position before moving.)}

Moi lan buoc tren con duong chua chac nhung dung hon, ho thay no khong de, nhung long nhe hon vi khong con phai song trong viec tron tranh.
\textit{(Each step on a path that is less certain but more truthful is not easier, yet it feels lighter because it ends avoidance.)}

Luc do ho moi hieu: dieu an toan nhat khong phai la khong bao gio rung. Dieu an toan nhat la song mot doi song minh khong can lien tuc tu thuyet phuc rang no van on.
\textit{(Then they understand: the safest thing is not never shaking. The safest thing is living a life you do not need to constantly convince yourself is fine.)}

\end{truyen}

\begin{baihoc}
Chac chan khong phai luc nao cung la dung. Doi khi, su thanh that voi minh moi la noi bat dau cua binh yen.
\textit{(Certainty is not always right. Sometimes honesty with yourself is where peace truly begins.)}
\end{baihoc}

\begin{ghinhoanh}
\textbf{Short English to remember:}

Certainty is not always right.

Sometimes honesty with yourself is where peace begins.
