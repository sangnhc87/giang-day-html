\section{Steve Jobs --- Stay Hungry, Stay Foolish}
\begin{truyen}{Steve Jobs --- Stay Hungry, Stay Foolish}{Steve Jobs --- Hoa Ky 2005}
\chuhoa{N}{}N am 2005, Steve Jobs dung truoc sinh vien tot nghiep Stanford va ke ba cau chuyen ve cuoc doi minh.\\
\textit{(In 2005, Steve Jobs stood before Stanford graduates and told three stories from his life.)}

Thu nhat: bi Apple sa thai la dieu tot nhat tung xay ra voi ong --- vi ta tu do de sang tao ra Pixar va Mac moi.\\
\textit{(First: being fired from Apple was the best thing that ever happened to him --- giving him freedom to create Pixar and the new Mac.)}

Thu hai: chan doan ung thu mang lai tinh giac --- 'thoi gian la huu han, dung song theo cuoc doi cua nguoi khac'.\\
\textit{(Second: his cancer diagnosis brought awakening --- 'time is finite, don't live by others' expectations.')}

Thu ba: 'Hay khat vong, hay ngo ngan' --- Stay hungry, stay foolish --- la ten goi cua long dam me chua the giam.\\
\textit{(Third: 'Stay hungry, stay foolish' --- the name for a passion that cannot be diminished.)}

Apple truoc o sau thoi ky ong quay lai la cong ty gia tri nhat the gioi --- vi Jobs da xay dung no bang chien luoc dua tren tri tue va dam me.\\
\textit{(Apple before and after his return became the world's most valuable company --- because Jobs built it on a strategy grounded in vision and passion.)}

\end{truyen}
\begin{baihoc}
  Nhung khuc quanh dau don trong cuoc doi khong chi la mat mat --- chung thuong la cua mo dan den noi ban can phai den.\\
  \textit{(Painful turning points in life are not only losses --- they are often doors opening to where you were always meant to go.)}
\end{baihoc}

\section{Walt Disney --- Bi Sa Thai Roi Tro Thanh Bieu Tuong}
\begin{truyen}{Walt Disney --- Bi Sa Thai Roi Tro Thanh Bieu Tuong}{Walt Disney --- Hoa Ky 1919}
\chuhoa{N}{}N am 1919, Walter Disney bi editor to bao sa thai vi 'thieu suc sang tao va khong co y tuong hay'.\\
\textit{(In 1919, Walter Disney was fired by a newspaper editor for 'lacking creativity and having no good ideas.')}

Ong ra di lam hoat hinh --- bi phat san lan thu nhat khi mua quyen hop dong roi mat tro lai truoc khi hoan thanh.\\
\textit{(He went on to make cartoons --- went bankrupt a first time when the studio buying his contract collapsed before completion.)}

Sau do ong tao ra Oswald the Rabbit --- roi mat ban quyen vi khong biet luat kinh doanh.\\
\textit{(Then he created Oswald the Rabbit --- then lost the copyright because he didn't understand business law.)}

Tren chuyen tau ve la, ong phac thao mot nhan vat moi --- chuot Mickey --- tren day giay an. Vo ong la ban dau tien xem.\\
\textit{(On the train home he sketched a new character --- Mickey Mouse --- on scrap paper. His wife was the first to see it.)}

Disney ngay nay la de che giai tri gia tri 200 ty do la --- xay tren nen tang mot nhan vat phac thao tren chuyen tau sau that bai.\\
\textit{(Disney today is an entertainment empire worth 200 billion dollars --- built on the foundation of a character sketched on a train after failure.)}

\end{truyen}
\begin{baihoc}
  Biet sa thai --- du dau khi ban dang thua --- nhieu khi la bat dau cua con duong dung, chi la muon hon ban du dinh.\\
  \textit{(Being fired --- painful at the moment --- is often the start of the right path, only later than you planned.)}
\end{baihoc}

\section{Oprah Winfrey --- Tu Ngheo Kho Den Bieu Tuong TG}
\begin{truyen}{Oprah Winfrey --- Tu Ngheo Kho Den Bieu Tuong TG}{Oprah Winfrey --- Hoa Ky 1954}
\chuhoa{O}{}O prah Winfrey sinh ra trong ngheo tung, lon len bi bao hanh, bi te nan o tuoi tho --- nhung ba con lai va bac len.\\
\textit{(Oprah Winfrey was born in poverty, grew up with abuse, experienced childhood trauma --- but she endured and rose.)}

O tuoi 19, ba duoc nhan lam phat thanh vien --- roi bi sa thai khi lam truyen hinh vi chi 'qua xuc cam voi cac chu de'.\\
\textit{(At 19 she was hired as a news anchor --- then fired from television for being 'too emotionally involved with her subjects.')}

'Qua xuc cam' chinh xac tro thanh suc manh lon nhat cua ba khi ba tao ra chuong trinh talkshow Oprah.\\
\textit{('Too emotional' became precisely her greatest strength when she created The Oprah Winfrey Show.)}

Chuong trinh len song 25 nam --- 1986 den 2011 --- tro thanh chuong trinh truyen hinh ban ngay xuat sac nhat lich su My.\\
\textit{(The show aired 25 years --- 1986 to 2011 --- becoming the highest-rated daytime TV show in American history.)}

Ba noi: 'Dieu lon nhat trong cuoc doi la dat hon ban la chinh ban --- la ke chuyen den cua cuoc doi chinh minh.'\\
\textit{(She said: 'The biggest adventure you can take is to live the life of your dreams --- you are the storyteller of your own life.')}

\end{truyen}
\begin{baihoc}
  Diem yeu ban bi che bai nhat doi khi chinh la the manh ban can khai thac nhat --- chi can dat no dung hoan canh.\\
  \textit{(The weakness you are most criticized for is often the strength you most need to develop --- just put it in the right context.)}
\end{baihoc}

\section{Thomas Edison --- Kho Tang Cua Su Kien Nhan}
\begin{truyen}{Thomas Edison --- Kho Tang Cua Su Kien Nhan}{Thomas Edison --- Hoa Ky 1847}
\chuhoa{T}{}T homas Edison khong chi sang tac den dien --- ong sang tac ra phong thi nghiem hien dai.\\
\textit{(Thomas Edison didn't just invent the light bulb --- he invented the modern research laboratory.)}

Ong xay dung Menlo Park --- 'xuong chui sang tao' dau tien tren the gioi, voi nhom nha khoa hoc lam viec cung nhau theo ke hoach.\\
\textit{(He built Menlo Park --- the world's first 'invention factory,' with a team of scientists working together with a plan.)}

Ong noi: 'Thien tai la 1 phan tram cam hung va 99 phan tram mo hoi.'\\
\textit{(He said: 'Genius is one percent inspiration and ninety-nine percent perspiration.')}

Nhung phat minh cua ong lam thay doi cuoc song hang ngay: den dien, may phat am, may chieu phim, tram dien luc.\\
\textit{(His inventions transformed daily life: the light bulb, phonograph, movie projector, power station.)}

Den cuoi doi, ong co 1.093 bang sang che --- ky luc the gioi con co gia tri cho den ngay nay.\\
\textit{(By the end of his life he held 1,093 patents --- a world record still standing today.)}

\end{truyen}
\begin{baihoc}
  Sang tao khong chi la tai nang --- do la qua trinh co to chuc, kien nhan va lam viec dau tu day di den cung.\\
  \textit{(Innovation is not merely talent --- it is an organized, patient process of working all the way to the end.)}
\end{baihoc}

\section{J.K. Rowling --- Viet Trong Quan Ca Phe}
\begin{truyen}{J.K. Rowling --- Viet Trong Quan Ca Phe}{J.K. Rowling --- Anh 1994}
\chuhoa{S}{}S au khi ly hon va mat viec, J.K. Rowling song nho tro cap xa hoi o Edinburgh voi con gai nho cua ba.\\
\textit{(After her divorce and job loss, J.K. Rowling lived on welfare in Edinburgh with her young daughter.)}

Moi sang, sau khi cho con di ngu, ba day xe ra quan ca phe --- vi benh nha re hon benh sua tam tu dien.\\
\textit{(Every morning after putting her daughter to sleep, she wheeled her stroller to a cafe --- because the heat was cheaper than heating the apartment.)}

Ba viet ban thao cuon Harry Potter trong quan ca phe do o nhiet do an toan --- viet tren may gay nhat co the tim.\\
\textit{(She wrote her Harry Potter manuscript in that cafe at warm temperature --- writing on paper scraps she could find.)}

Cuoi sach ba lam 60 ban sao voi may in nha --- vi khong du tien mua giay copy du luong.\\
\textit{(At the book's end she made 60 photocopies on a rented machine --- not having enough money for sufficient copy paper.)}

Cuon sach dau tien do om co ban duoc 500 trieu ban, Harry Potter tro thanh thuong hieu trieu do la.\\
\textit{(That cramped first book sold 500 million copies; Harry Potter became a multi-billion dollar brand.)}

\end{truyen}
\begin{baihoc}
  Ban thao vi dai nhat cua ban co the dang duoc viet trong hoat canh kho khat nhat --- kho khat doi khi la phuong phap luong tot nhat cho su sang tao.\\
  \textit{(Your greatest manuscript may be being written in your most difficult circumstances --- hardship is sometimes the best fertilizer for creativity.)}
\end{baihoc}

\section{Elon Musk --- Ho Tho Cuoi Cung Truoc Boc Cua}
\begin{truyen}{Elon Musk --- Ho Tho Cuoi Cung Truoc Boc Cua}{Elon Musk --- Nam Phi/Hoa Ky 2008}
\chuhoa{N}{}N am 2008, Elon Musk dang lam hai cong ty cung luc: Tesla dang sat pha san va SpaceX vua mat chiec ten lua thu ba.\\
\textit{(In 2008, Elon Musk was running two companies simultaneously: Tesla was nearing bankruptcy and SpaceX had just lost its third rocket.)}

Ong bo tat ca tien ca nhan vao hai cong ty nay --- tat ca --- va bay gio sap mat het.\\
\textit{(He had put all his personal money into both companies --- everything --- and was about to lose it all.)}

Ong chia so: 'Toi thuc day nua ngay va khong the ngo duoc --- toi chi hieu tai sao Elon Musk khong ngu duoc.'\\
\textit{(He shared: 'I woke up at 3am and couldn't sleep --- I finally understood why Elon Musk couldn't sleep.')}

Ten lua thu tu cua SpaceX thanh cong --- va Tesla nhan duoc von dau tu lu'ot song cuoi nam.\\
\textit{(SpaceX's fourth rocket succeeded --- and Tesla received a last-minute investment in late that year.)}

Bay gio mot trong hai cong ty la lon nhat the gioi theo tung linh vuc cua minh --- nhung o nam 2008, chi co mot su lua chon la tiep tuc.\\
\textit{(Today each company is among the world's largest in its field --- but in 2008, there was only one option: to continue.)}

\end{truyen}
\begin{baihoc}
  Thuong chi can them mot lan thu nua sau tat ca cac lan thu truoc --- nhung ca gan lam, nen co gang than chon su kien nhan len tren he that vong.\\
  \textit{(It often takes just one more try after all previous tries --- the trick is choosing patience over despair, even when hope seems gone.)}
\end{baihoc}

\section{Warren Buffett --- Kien Nhan La Vo Khi Bi Mat}
\begin{truyen}{Warren Buffett --- Kien Nhan La Vo Khi Bi Mat}{Warren Buffett --- Hoa Ky TK 20}
\chuhoa{W}{}W arren Buffett bat dau dau tu tu luc 11 tuoi --- va giu co phieu dau tien cua minh qua nhung bien dong lon.\\
\textit{(Warren Buffett started investing at age 11 --- and held his first stock through major market swings.)}

Triet ly dau tu cua ong don gian den muc ai cung biet: 'Mua co phieu cua cong ty tot va giu chu khong ban.'\\
\textit{(His investment philosophy is so simple everyone knows it: 'Buy stock in good companies and simply hold, don't sell.')}

Nhung su don gian nay doi hoi mot dieu hiem hoi: kien nhan muoi nam, 20 nam, 30 nam.\\
\textit{(But this simplicity demands something rare: patience for 10 years, 20 years, 30 years.)}

Nam tuoi 90, Buffett van di lam moi ngay, van doc 5-6 tia tuc kinh te moi buoi sang.\\
\textit{(At age 90, Buffett still goes to work every day, still reads 5-6 financial newspapers every morning.)}

Ong noi: '99 phan tram tai san cua toi duoc tao ra sau tuoi 50 --- vi toi co du kien nhan cho lai suat kep lam viec.'\\
\textit{(He said: '99 percent of my wealth was created after age 50 --- because I had enough patience to let compound interest work.')}

\end{truyen}
\begin{baihoc}
  Lai kep --- trong dau tu hay trong hoc tap --- can thoi gian de phat huy suc manh; kien nhan khong phai la chon dung ma la vu khi tich cuc.\\
  \textit{(Compound interest --- in investment or in learning --- needs time to unleash its power; patience is not passive waiting but an active weapon.)}
\end{baihoc}

\section{Ho Chi Minh --- Hanh Trinh 30 Nam Tim Duong Cuu Nuoc}
\begin{truyen}{Ho Chi Minh --- Hanh Trinh 30 Nam Tim Duong Cuu Nuoc}{Ho Chi Minh --- Viet Nam TK 20}
\chuhoa{N}{}N am 1911, Nguyen Tat Thanh --- 21 tuoi --- roi Bo Me Ha Noi tren mot con tau lam bep di tim duong giai phong dat nuoc.\\
\textit{(In 1911, Nguyen Tat Thanh --- 21 years old --- left the port of Saigon on a ship as a cook, going to find a path to liberate his country.)}

Ong song va lam viec o nhieu nuoc: Anh, Phap, My, Nga, Trung Quoc --- lam nhieu nghe khac nhau de ton tai trong khi hoc hoi.\\
\textit{(He lived and worked in many countries: England, France, America, Russia, China --- taking many jobs to survive while learning.)}

Tai Phap, ong tham gia phong trao cong nhan va dang Cong san, gui ban yeu sach len Hoa hoi Hoa binh Paris --- khong ai de y.\\
\textit{(In France he joined the workers' movement and the Communist Party, submitted a petition to the Paris Peace Conference --- no one noticed.)}

Sau ba muoi nam --- 1941 --- ong moi ve nuoc, lap Mat tran Viet Minh va lanh dao cach mang thanh cong nam 1945.\\
\textit{(After thirty years --- in 1941 --- he finally returned home, established the Viet Minh Front, and led the successful revolution of 1945.)}

Ong noi: 'Khong co gi quy hon doc lap tu do' --- cau noi cua mot nguoi da dang ca cuoc doi de ket luan dieu do.\\
\textit{(He said: 'Nothing is more precious than independence and freedom' --- words of a man who spent his entire life to reach that conclusion.)}

\end{truyen}
\begin{baihoc}
  Anh cua viet nam cuoc doi --- hanh trinh 30 nam --- chung minh rang nhung muc tieu lon can thoi gian lon; dung do so qua thoi gian ma bo cuoc.\\
  \textit{(Uncle Ho's life journey --- 30 years --- proves that great goals require great time; don't abandon your goal fearing the passage of time.)}
\end{baihoc}

\section{Marie Curie --- Hai Giai Nobel, Mot Nguoi Phu Nu}
\begin{truyen}{Marie Curie --- Hai Giai Nobel, Mot Nguoi Phu Nu}{Marie Curie --- Ba Lan/Phap 1903}
\chuhoa{M}{}M arie Curie vi sinh ra la phu nu o the ki XIX, khong duoc vao dai hoc o Ba Lan --- phu nu khong duoc hoc.\\
\textit{(Marie Curie, born female in the 19th century, was denied university education in Poland --- women were not admitted.)}

Ba theo hoc tin hoc tai Paris --- song trong phong thue lanh buot, mac ao dong cuoi chua, an banh mi qua ngay.\\
\textit{(She attended secret lectures in Paris --- living in a freezing rented room, wearing the cheapest winter coat, eating bread to get through the day.)}

Ba dong chung voi Pierre Curie --- mot nha khoa hoc cung say me nghien cuu --- va ca hai kham pha ra Polonium va Radium.\\
\textit{(She collaborated with Pierre Curie --- a fellow scientist equally passionate about research --- and together they discovered Polonium and Radium.)}

Nam 1903, ba tro thanh nguoi phu nu dau tien nhan Nobel Vat ly; nam 1911, nhan Nobel Hoa hoc - nguoi duy nhat nhan ca hai.\\
\textit{(In 1903 she became the first woman to receive the Nobel Prize in Physics; in 1911 the Nobel in Chemistry --- the only person to receive both.)}

Vien Hoc Vien Khoa Hoc Phap tu choi ba vi la phu nu trong nhieu nam --- nhung hoc thuyet khoa hoc cua ba thi khong ai tu choi duoc.\\
\textit{(The French Academy of Sciences refused her membership for years because she was a woman --- but no one could refuse her scientific discoveries.)}

\end{truyen}
\begin{baihoc}
  Rao can xa hoi khong phai cai chet cua uoc mo --- chung chi la vat can cuoc duong chay. Nhu'ng nguoi chon chay tiep la nhung nguoi thay doi lich su.\\
  \textit{(Social barriers are not the death of dreams --- they are only hurdles on the race course. Those who choose to keep running are the ones who change history.)}
\end{baihoc}

\section{Nguyen Truong To --- Nha Cai Cach Viet Nam TK 19}
\begin{truyen}{Nguyen Truong To --- Nha Cai Cach Viet Nam TK 19}{Nguyen Truong To --- Viet Nam 1863}
\chuhoa{N}{}N guyen Truong To la mot tri thuc Viet Nam Cong giao, di Phap va Italia, the ky XIX, va nhin thay su phat trien cua phuong Tay.\\
\textit{(Nguyen Truong To was a Vietnamese Catholic intellectual who traveled to France and Italy in the 19th century and witnessed Western development.)}

Ong gui len trieu Nguyen 58 ban diec nghi ve canh tan --- canh tan giao duc, quan doi, thuong mai, ngoai giao.\\
\textit{(He submitted 58 memoranda to the Nguyen court calling for reform --- reforming education, military, trade, and diplomacy.)}

Trieu dinh nghe --- roi khong lam gi hoac lam qua cham; va Phap da xam luoc truoc khi bat ky cai cach nao duoc thuc hien.\\
\textit{(The court listened --- then did nothing or acted too slowly; and France invaded before any reform was implemented.)}

Ong viet: 'Nu'oc muon manh thi nuoc phai hoc, phai thay, phai tien --- khong the nhin the gioi thay doi con minh duc'\\
\textit{(He wrote: 'A nation wanting strength must learn, must change, must advance --- one cannot watch the world change while standing still.')}

Ong mat o tuoi 38, khi cuoc canh tan chu'a kip bat dau --- nhung tu tuong cua ong van con song cho den ngay nay.\\
\textit{(He died at 38, when reform had barely begun --- but his ideas live on to this day.)}

\end{truyen}
\begin{baihoc}
  Kien taO cuoc doi khong chi la kien tao ban than --- nguoi co tam nhin cung kien tao xa hoi va the he sau, du ho khong song de thay ket qua.\\
  \textit{(Building one's life is not only building oneself --- those with vision also build society and future generations, even if they don't live to see the result.)}
\end{baihoc}
